The FEP Operations Center receives claims in the 837 Health Care Claim transaction format from Plans. Plans can send Dental, Professional and Institutional claims using industry standard formats. During the process of translating 837 Health Care Claim transactions from the X12 standard format to the FEP Claim Record (FCR) format used by the FEPExpress Claims Processing system (FEPExpress), a limited number of edits will be performed. The editing ensures that data is not lost due to FEP system limitations. The editing also ensures that data is compatible for all FCRs for a single claim.

These edits are focused on Coordination of Benefits (COB) information that can be reported on the 837. The edits listed below result in a claim being translated from the X12 format to the FCR format and then deferred in FEPExpress. Plans have the option of:

  • Correcting the COB data that is reported on the 837 and submitting the updated 837 Health Care transaction to the FEPOC; or
  • Reviewing and correcting the COB data via the FEPDirect application. It should be noted that both Amount Paid other Carrier fields and OPR (Other Payer Record) data should be reviewed and updated as necessary.
