Logging Operational Events In Administration

Purpose

Similar to logging access to HL7 messages using audit logging, which is the process of recording what message data was viewed by each engine user, it is often important to log specific operational events that occur in Administration.

The following operational events are logged in the Engine Monitor log:

  • Logging in or out of Administration
  • Starting or stopping the engine service
  • Taking action upon a connection (e.g., starting, stopping, or restarting a connection; suspending or resuming alerts)
  • Clearing messages from a queue
  • Resending a message from a connection log
  • Setting or Resetting Base Preferences
  • Resetting user preferences to use default preferences

Procedure

  1. Access and view the Engine Monitor log.

  2. Filter the Engine Monitor log, as desired.
